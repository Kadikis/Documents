About Countora

Countora is a powerful counting and tracking application designed to transform the way you record and analyze data. Whether you're tracking attendance, monitoring inventory, or documenting habits, Countora provides detailed insights while maintaining your privacy.

Key Features

  • Detailed timestamp tracking for every count
  • Privacy-focused design with local data storage
  • Intuitive user interface for easy counting and tracking
  • Comprehensive analytics and pattern recognition
  • No account required - start counting immediately
  • Data stays on your device - no cloud storage

Privacy Commitment

At Countora, we take your privacy seriously. Our app:

  • Stores all data locally on your device
  • Never collects personal information
  • Operates without requiring user accounts
