About Intervalist Workout Timer

Intervalist Workout Timer is an interval and workout timer app that helps you build and run custom routines with precise timers and voice cues. Create workouts from your exercise library, attach custom intervals (work, rest, cooldown), and follow along with audio announcements—all with a privacy-focused, offline-first design.

Key Features

  • Custom interval timers with work, rest, and cooldown—countdown or stopwatch
  • Workout management: build and organize routines with exercises and intervals
  • Exercise library with categories, muscle groups, and built-in or custom exercises
  • Voice announcements for exercise and interval changes
  • Local-only data storage—works fully offline
  • No account required—start creating workouts immediately
  • Clean, intuitive interface for focused training

Privacy Commitment

We take your privacy seriously. The app:

  • Stores all data locally on your device only (no cloud sync)
  • Works completely offline—no internet required
  • Does not collect or transmit personal information
  • Requires no user accounts, registrations, or sign-ups
  • No analytics, tracking, or data collection of any kind
