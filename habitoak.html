About HabitOak

HabitOak is a powerful habit tracking application designed to help you build and maintain positive habits. Track your daily routines, monitor your progress, and develop lasting behavioral changes with our privacy-focused, offline-first approach.

Key Features

  • Track daily habits with simple check-offs
  • Detailed progress analytics and streak tracking
  • Completely offline - works without internet connection
  • Privacy-focused design with local data storage only
  • No account required - start tracking habits immediately
  • Beautiful, intuitive interface for effortless habit building

Privacy Commitment

At HabitOak, we take your privacy seriously. Our app:

  • Works completely offline - no internet required
  • Stores all habit data locally on your device only
  • Never collects or transmits personal information
  • No user accounts, registrations, or sign-ups required
  • No analytics, tracking, or data collection of any kind
